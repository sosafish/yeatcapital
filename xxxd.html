Dropping the Primitive Whitepaper!!

OG Thread by @0xEstelle - Oct 24, 2021

After months of work and the collaborative effort of about a dozen contributors, we're excited to introduce Primitive's RMM-01: replicating covered calls with CFMM shares! Partying face Primitive RMM-01

A direct extension of the work of RMMs Replicating Market Makers by Guillermo Angeris Alex Evans Tarun Chitra, we implement a CFMM whose liquidity shares probabilistically replicate the payoff of a Black-Scholes covered call.

First off, why approach covered calls this way?? The key advantages are:

1. Premiums w/o the need for counterparties

2.No oracles

3.Composable DeFi primitive

4.Any token pair

5.Deep liquidity concentration in at-the-money pools

The ability to sell calls and collect premiums without the need for a counterparty is ensured simply through the swap fee of the AMM. This is probabilistic but can be optimized to capture more revenue. You'll never need to deal with this guy.

Another more subtle implication: pools nearer to their strike and expiry exhibit much deeper concentration of liquidity, making swaps more efficient. By shorting LP tokens and paying a premium, you can obtain the same payoff as buying an option. Closing the position is as simple as re-minting and returning the LP tokens.



Alternatively, skip the shorting entirely and go long cash-or-nothing calls or asset-or-nothing puts just by paying a premium. Same curve different payoffs. These are just two examples of potentially many options-like payoffs that can be derived from RMM-01. Each approach comes with a distinct set of tradeoffs and we're actively exploring the design space for payoffs that provide the most flexibility.

We've had a ton of fun exploring these topics over the past months and we're super excited to share the results of our efforts with the greater defi community.
